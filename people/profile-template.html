Biography

George Ball was born in Romford in 1888 to Frederick Ball and Ethel Rhoda Smith. A railway clerk by profession, he was known locally for his stern manner and impeccable handwriting. He married Ethel in 1913 and the two remained together for more than sixty years.

He lived most of his adult life in Brentwood and served on the parish council for many years. His descendants regard him as the quiet patriarch of the family.
